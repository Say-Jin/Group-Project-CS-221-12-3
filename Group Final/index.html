Our mission is to provide a way and introduce people to new foods based off their music taste.


The way Food Fiktion works is we utilize a tool in music called the camelot scale. The camelot scale


uses colors to help djs or even musicians find similar music and keys. Based off the color of the song


a user inputs we find out a persons taste in food, mood, and music taste. We hope to elevate the way


people think of food and music. Food is an art and so is music, Food Fiktion!
